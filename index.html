About Us

Discover innovation at PESU Electronic City. We're your gateway to a world where robotics and automation ignite brilliance. Craft robots, automate wonders – that's our vibe.
Robotics? It's the art of design, construction, and magic. Automation? It's tech choreography with a human touch. Journey from ancient whispers to Unimate's '60s debut, then leap to PLC-powered '70s and microprocessor-driven '80s.
The 2000s? Collaborative bots and AI sizzle. Fast-forward, and AI propels us into a future of unmatched efficiency. Here, we're your stage, where practical skills shine and knowledge soars. Step in, stand out – welcome to limitless innovation!
